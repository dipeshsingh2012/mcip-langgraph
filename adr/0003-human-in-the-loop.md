# ADR 0003: Human-in-the-Loop by Default for Clinical Outputs

Date: 2026-07-07
Status: Accepted

Context
-------
Clinical outputs produced by LLM agents may affect patient care; regulatory and safety requirements demand clinician oversight.

Decision
--------
Require human review and approval for all clinical outputs generated by MCIP agents before they are considered final.

Consequences
------------
- Pros: Improves patient safety, aligns with compliance and regulatory expectations, increases clinician trust.
- Cons: Adds latency and requires UX/workflow design to make review efficient.

Alternatives Considered
-----------------------
- Allow certain low-risk outputs to be auto-approved (future consideration with strong validation).
- Fully autonomous outputs (rejected for MVP due to safety concerns).
